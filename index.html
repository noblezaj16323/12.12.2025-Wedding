Frequently Asked Questions

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP no later than November 12, 2025.

What time should I Arrive?

The Ceremony will start at 2PM. Please arrive at least 30 minutes or an hour earlier to the venue so that you could get settled before the proceedings.

Can I bring a guest/date?

If your guest name is listed on your invite, then yes! Otherwise, we would love to keep the wedding as intimate as possible with close friends and family.

Are kids allowed?

We decided to keep our wedding and reception an adults-only event.

Can I bring a gift to the wedding?

Your love and presence is the greatest gift we could ask for. If you'd like to bless us with something more, a monetary gift would be greatly appreciated.

Is there parking available?

Yes, complimentary parking is available at the venue. We recommend arriving early to secure a spot.
